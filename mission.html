Who we are



stepon Education is an Educational Consultancy situated at bhainsepati – Kathmandu, which is regarded as the hub for students trying to get admissions in foreign colleges and universities. Since 2019, our organization has been assisting the prospective undergraduates, graduates and other professionals in terms of getting admission in foreign colleges and universities that match their needs. To this date, we have sent hundreds of deserving students to uk, canada, germany and finland.

We provide comprehensive information to assist students in identifying their preferred courses and to set their academic goals. Based on the student’s academic goals, we develop options for their educational path.

We act as the representatives for a number of foreign colleges and universities, while also evaluating admissions on their behalf.

Vision



We look at the trend of overseas education as an indispensible phenomenon of the entire globalization process. Students learn to adapt into a new environment, enhance the ability to observe from the cross cultural differences and sharpen their knowledge base by observing the advancement in world technology.

With the multinational companies holding dominant positions in the corporate world globally, overseas education can equip the students with enhanced abilities to perform their tasks as successful global employees.

Objective



  • To offer boundless educational opportunities to Nepalese students in universities/colleges around the globe through complete abroad study programs.

  • To enable students to select the best universities/colleges according to their academic requirements and financial credentials by providing all the supportive information they require.

  • To make available quality and accurate information about colleges/universities and academic programs.

  • To provide Admission counseling, Documentation assistance, Visa guidance and related services.
